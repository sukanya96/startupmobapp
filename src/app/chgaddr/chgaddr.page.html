1. What documents are required for Office Address Change?

New Address Proof, Board Resolution , NOC for Shifting of Registered Office Declaration for Shifting of Registered Office.

2. What is the fees and charges for Shifting of Office Address?

Rs.300 is the Form INC 22 s filed within prescribed time limit.

3. What Forms to be filed for changing the Company Address?

Form INC -22.

4. What is time limit to file change of office address documents with ROC?

The time limit is 15 days from passing of the Board Resolution for Shifting of Registered Office.

5. What actions need to be taken after shifting of registered office address of Company?

The Name Plate mentioning the address has to be modified, the Letterhead of the Company has to be changed, the Statutory Register has to be shifted to the new registered office, Shop Act License & PAN card need to be altered.

6. Can registered office be situated in foreign country?

No.

7. What are different types of Registered Office Shifting?

a) Within the Local Limits of the City b) Within the limits of the same ROC c) Within the same state but different ROC (Only in case of Maharashtra and Tamil Nadu) d) From one state to another
